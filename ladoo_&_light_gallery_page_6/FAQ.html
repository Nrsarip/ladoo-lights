Booking & Process

How far in advance should we book your services?

expand_more

We recommend booking as early as possible, especially for peak wedding seasons (October to March). Ideally, 6-12 months in advance ensures we can dedicate our full attention to your date. However, we do accommodate last-minute requests if our calendar permits.

Do you offer initial consultations?

expand_more

Yes! We offer a complimentary initial consultation, either virtually or in-person at our studio. This allows us to understand your vision, discuss themes, and see if we are the right fit for your special day.

Can you work with a specific theme I have in mind?

expand_more

Absolutely. Customization is at the heart of Ladoo & Light. Whether you envision a traditional royal aesthetic, a bohemian floral setup, or a modern minimalist vibe, we tailor every detail to match your unique theme and color palette.

Services & Decor

Do you provide fresh flowers only?

expand_more

We specialize in fresh floral arrangements to provide the most fragrant and luxurious experience. However, we also offer high-quality silk florals for specific installations where durability or weather conditions might be a concern, such as outdoor ceiling drapes in summer.

What other decor items do you supply besides flowers?

expand_more

Beyond florals, we provide comprehensive decor solutions including lighting (chandeliers, fairy lights), drapery, furniture rentals (sofas for stage, chiavari chairs), props, table centerpieces, and custom signage.

Logistics & Setup

Do you handle setup and teardown?

expand_more

Yes, our full-service package includes delivery, professional setup, and post-event teardown. We coordinate directly with your venue to ensure seamless logistics so you can enjoy your event stress-free.

Do you travel for destination weddings?

expand_more

Yes! We love destination weddings. We are based in Mumbai but frequently travel to Jaipur, Udaipur, Goa, and Kerala. Travel and accommodation fees for our team will apply for events outside our base city.

Pricing & Payment

What is your minimum budget?

expand_more

Our bespoke floral and decor services typically start from INR 2,00,000 for intimate events. For full-scale wedding production involving multiple functions (Sangeet, Wedding, Reception), budgets vary based on complexity and guest count. Please contact us for a personalized quote.

What are your payment terms?

expand_more

We require a 40% non-refundable retainer to secure your date. A second payment of 40% is due 30 days before the event, and the final 20% balance is due 1 week prior to the event date.
