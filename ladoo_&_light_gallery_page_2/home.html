Rajasthani Royal Sangeet

Inspired by the resplendence of Amer Fort, this sangeet featured carved jaali panels, marigold cascades, and a canopy of hand-beaded lights that shimmered to the rhythm of live dhol.

Guests were greeted with attar-scented pathways, bespoke lounge baithaks, and a statement floral chandelier evoking royal durbars—an unforgettable night of music and mehfil.
