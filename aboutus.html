Our Story

Weaving Tradition with Modern Elegance

Ladoo & Light isn't just about decoration; it's about creating the sacred atmosphere where your new life begins.

From a Single Marigold to Majestic Mandaps

Ladoo & Light began with a simple belief: every Indian wedding is a unique story waiting to be told through color, fragrance, and light. What started as a small boutique floral service in the bylanes of Jaipur has blossomed into a premier event styling agency known for its attention to detail and reverence for tradition.

The name "Ladoo" represents the sweetness of new beginnings and the traditional warmth of Indian hospitality, while "Light" signifies the illumination of love and the contemporary flair we bring to every venue.
